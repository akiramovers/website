No entry of medium, heavy vehicles into Delhi NCR

In September 2021, the Government of Delhi had implemented a "no entry" restriction for heavy commercial vehicles during specific time periods in Delhi-NCR (National Capital Region) to control traffic congestion and improve air quality. The restriction on heavy vehicles was enforced during peak traffic hours in the morning and evening. The specific timings for the "no entry" restriction on heavy vehicles can vary, and it is advisable to refer to the official government sources or check with the local traffic authorities for the most up-to-date information regarding these regulations. Additionally, the restriction may also be subject to exemptions for certain types of vehicles, such as those carrying essential commodities or vehicles used for public services. To ensure compliance with the heavy vehicle "no entry" rules in Delhi-NCR, it is recommended to plan travel accordingly and avoid driving heavy commercial vehicles during the specified restricted hours. Violating these restrictions may result in fines and penalties imposed by the traffic authorities.
